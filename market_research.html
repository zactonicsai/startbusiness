What is Market Research & Competitive Analysis?

Market research helps you find customers for your business. Competitive analysis helps you make your business unique. Combine them to find a competitive advantage for your small business.

Market Research explores:

  • • Who your customers are (segments, personas)
  • • Their needs, pains, and “jobs-to-be-done”
  • • Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM) and demand
  • • Where customers search and buy (channels)

Competitive Analysis looks at:

  • • Direct & indirect competitors
  • • Differentiators, pricing, and positioning
  • • Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats (SWOT)
  • • Gaps you can fill for an advantage
